Uber leadership

Our leaders are committed to simplifying lives and building an inspiring organisation while impacting the region’s communities. Careem was started by Mudassir Sheikha and Magnus Olsson - both ex-management consultants looking to solve the problem of the region’s lack of reliable transportation. In 2013, Abdullah Elyas became Careem’s third co-founder, and together, over a decade, they led the company into 14 countries and hired over 12 thousand colleagues to bring their vision to life
